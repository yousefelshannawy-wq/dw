| sp2           | 3                       | Trigonal Planar | 120         |
| sp3           | 4                       | Tetrahedral     | 109.5       |
| sp3d          | 5                       | Trigonal Bipyramidal | 90, 120    |
| sp3d2         | 6                       | Octahedral      | 90          |
